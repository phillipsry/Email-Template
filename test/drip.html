Hot Coffey Design was founded out of the need for businesses to have a one-stop creative agency where they could get access to all of the marketing resources and tools they need to be successful and stay ahead. We appreciate you choosing us and look forward to helping you by providing you the best in the services we have to offer!

Now, before we get started on your project we will need a few things from you first. This will help us to better understand your business and give us what we need to move forward.

Below is a list of some items that we require to get started on your project:

  1. Your logo in vector format if possible

    – If we are creating the logo then we are good on this one!

  2. Your business details


    -About your company
    -What you do
    -What you sell
    -What services you provide
    -Your hours of operation
    -Your location - can people come to your offices to see examples and purchase your services?
    -Your phone, fax and email address you would like to use for this business
    -Your social network links – if you have them set up

  3. Pictures

    -We need all the pictures you have for your products/services
    -All should be categorized in folders
    -Descriptions of those images

  4. Any and all marketing material you have used

    – ex. brochures, etc.

  5. Your target audience

    – the people you are wanting to reach

  6. Color schemes you like


  7. Visual examples of what you like

    – 2 or 3 whether getting a website or logo

  8. Style Preference

    – ex. corporate, creative, sophisticated or clean

  9. Type of website

    – one page, multi-page or no preference (if required)
